Every day life at MondaySunday

Our universe is a place where true treasures
Of the everyday life are valued.

It could be daydreaming on a monday, drinking champagne
On a tuesday or bringing nature inside on a wednesday .
We love to create small miracles, so the everyday moments
Become visible and adored. Life is now - so let’s hold hands
On a thursday, dance naked on a friday, refurnish on
A saturday or seek new stories on a sunday.

Let’s create a space where everything is possible.
Let’s discover a new world together.
A world of neverending stories from monday to sunday.
